Pine Hill Fire Company No.1., Inc.

100% Volunteer. 24/7 Response.

When the alarm sounds, we respond. Day or night. No exceptions.

We are a volunteer fire company serving Pine Hill and surrounding communities. Our members respond to fires, vehicle accidents, and emergency calls every day of the year.

We do this with no paid staff. Every response is your neighbor showing up.
Who We Are
What We Do

We handle real calls. Real emergencies. Real consequences.

🧯

Fire Suppression

We respond to structure fires, chimney fires, vehicle fires, and wildland incidents. Our crews train year-round to operate safely and aggressively when it matters.

🚑

Emergency Response

We support medical calls, vehicle accidents, and mutual aid incidents across the region. We work alongside EMS and neighboring departments to stabilize scenes quickly.

🏘️

Community Protection

We are funded by a mix of town support and community donations. Every dollar goes back into equipment, training, and keeping this company operational.
